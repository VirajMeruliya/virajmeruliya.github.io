Research

My research focused on understanding the properties of Quantum Field Theories and their connection to Gravitational Physics. My research lies in the field of High Energy Physics and some of the topics that I am interested in are: Conformal Field Theories, Low-Dimensional theories of gravity, Gauge-Gravity Duality, Black Holes and Information Loss.
